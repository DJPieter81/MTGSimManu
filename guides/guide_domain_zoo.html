Metagame Strategy — Non-Obvious Findings
Damage ≠ kills: Scion of Draco deals the most damage (996) but Ragavan, Nimble Pilferer gets the most kills (51). The closer and the damage engine are different cards — don't evaluate them the same way.
Speed shapes your closer: In fast matchups (T≤6) like Izzet Prowes, your Ragavan closes. In grindy matchups (T≥8) like Azorius Cont, Phlage takes over. Board differently for speed vs grind.
Sideboard asymmetry: vs 4c Omnath, G1 WR is 100% but match WR drops to 56% (-44pp). Opponent adapts better post-board.
