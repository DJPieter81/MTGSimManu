## Metagame Observations (April 2026)

The current MTGO Modern metagame shows several notable trends compared to our simulator's data.

**Boros Energy is the dominant deck**, not Domain Zoo. In the Challenge 64, Boros Energy took 3 of the top 4 spots and represented 18.75% of the field. In the April 5 league, it had 9 of 45 lists (20%). This contrasts sharply with our simulator where Domain Zoo was the runaway best deck at 93.6%.

**Domain Zoo is strong but not dominant.** It placed 5th-6th in the Challenge and had consistent 5-0 league finishes, but it is clearly a tier below Boros Energy in the real metagame. The real-world lists run 22 lands (not 26), use Leyline of the Guildpact (which our simulator doesn't model), and include Doorkeeper Thrull instead of Nishoba Brawler.

**Izzet Steel-Cutter is a new archetype** not present in our simulator at all. It placed 3rd and 8th in the Challenge with $2,000+ price tags, suggesting it uses expensive staples. This appears to be an evolution of Izzet Prowess.

**Affinity remains strong** at 15.56% of the league metagame (7 lists) and 9.38% of the Challenge field.

**Key cards not in our simulator:** Leyline of the Guildpact, Doorkeeper Thrull, Consign to Memory, Spelunking, Phelia Exuberant Shepherd, Casey Jones Vigilante, The Legend of Roku, Static Prison, Scapeshift (in Amulet), and Skateboard (in Affinity/Izzet).
